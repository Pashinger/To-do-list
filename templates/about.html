The features included:
  • Effortless creation, modification, and labeling of to-do lists
  • Users can register, log in, and manage their account details
  • Secure password recovery through email with token-based verification
  • Tasks can be reordered, customized with colors, or deleted
  • Personalized styling options for lists, including various backgrounds and fonts
  • Lists can be downloaded in PDF, JPG or PNG formats
  • Registered users can save their lists in a database for future modification, download, or deletion
  • Integration with ZenQuotes API for a dose of inspirational quotes
